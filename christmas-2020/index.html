May the Warmth of Our Hearts
Be with You This Season

For countless years, humans have come together in the darkest hours of the year to kindle the light of fellowship.

We eat and drink together, we give and receive together, we raise our voices together in song – because being together is what makes us human.

We will not allow this year to take that away from us.

We decide what being together means. And whether we share our stories through a screen, or over a phone, or by ink and paper, our humanity remains undiminished.

There’s no denying it’s been a hard year. But no matter how long the night, how far the distance, or how hard the road, the light of our fellowship shines bright against the darkness.

It’s the light that will lead us home.

All our love for a warm and joyful season.

Claire, Elias, and William
